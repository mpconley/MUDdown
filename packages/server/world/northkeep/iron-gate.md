# The Iron Gate

A massive portcullis of blackened iron hangs halfway open, its chains
**rusted** into place. The [gears of the winch](cmd:examine gears) gleam with
fresh oil, though no one seems to tend them. Beyond the gate, a dirt road
winds into misty hills.
