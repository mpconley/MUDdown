# Jeweler's Shop

A narrow shop with velvet-lined display cases. Dozens of rings, necklaces,
and brooches catch the lamplight. A magnifying lens mounted on an articulated
arm sits on the workbench, surrounded by tiny tools. The door has
[three heavy locks](cmd:examine locks).
