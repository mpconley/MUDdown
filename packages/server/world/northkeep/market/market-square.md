# Market Square

An open plaza ringed with colorful stalls and permanent shops. A central
well serves as a gathering point for merchants and customers alike. Canvas
awnings snap in the breeze, and a [juggler](npc:juggler) entertains
a small crowd near the well.

## Exits
- [West](go:west) — Market Entrance
- [North](go:north) — Jeweler's Shop
- [South](go:south) — Blacksmith's Forge

## Present
- A [juggler](npc:juggler) entertains a small crowd near the well.
- A [spice merchant](npc:spice-merchant) tends rows of aromatic jars.
- A [pickpocket](npc:pickpocket) lurks near the well, watching the crowd.

## Items
- A [silver coin](item:silver-coin) glints between the cobblestones.
