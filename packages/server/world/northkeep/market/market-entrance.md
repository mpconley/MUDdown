# Market Entrance

A wooden archway draped with faded pennants marks the entrance to Northkeep's
bustling market district. The clatter of carts and haggling voices echo off
the stone buildings. The smell of spices mingles with the yeasty aroma
drifting from the bakery behind you.

- [East](go:east) — Market Square
