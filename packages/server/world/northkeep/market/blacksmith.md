# Blacksmith's Forge

Waves of heat roll from the open forge. An enormous bellows creaks
rhythmically, feeding the coals to a white-hot glow. Racks of horseshoes,
nails, and blades line the soot-stained walls. The [anvil](cmd:examine anvil)
bears the dents of a thousand hammered days.

## Exits
- [North](go:north) — Market Square

## Present
- [Gorath the Smith](npc:gorath) hammers a red-hot blade on the anvil.

## Items
- A [dull shortsword](item:shortsword) leans against the tool rack.
- A set of [iron tongs](item:tongs) rests on the hearth.
