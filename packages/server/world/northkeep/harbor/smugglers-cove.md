# Smuggler's Cove

A hidden inlet beneath a rocky overhang. The water here is dark and still.
A half-rotted rowboat is pulled up on the gravel shore, and scratches on
the cave wall mark [crude tallies](cmd:examine tallies). The air is damp
and carries a faint whiff of contraband spices.

## Exits
- [East](go:east) — The Pier

## Items
- A [locked chest](item:locked-chest) is wedged between two boulders.
- A [torn map](item:torn-map) is pinned under a rock.
:::
