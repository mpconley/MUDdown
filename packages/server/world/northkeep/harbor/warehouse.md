# Warehouse

A cavernous stone building stacked high with crates and barrels. Dust motes
drift through shafts of light from high windows. The air smells of tar, salt,
and old rope. A [manifest board](cmd:examine manifest) is nailed to a support
beam, listing expected shipments.

## Exits
- [North](go:north) — Docks District
- [South](go:south) — Pier

## Present
- A [dock foreman](npc:foreman) checks off items on a clipboard.

## Items
- A [coil of rope](item:rope) sits atop a barrel.
- A [crowbar](item:crowbar) leans against a crate.
