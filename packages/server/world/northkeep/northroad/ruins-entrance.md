# Ruins Entrance

Crumbled stone columns flank a yawning doorway carved into the hillside.
Ivy and moss cling to what were once intricate carvings. A cold draft
blows from within, carrying the smell of wet stone. [Faded runes](cmd:examine runes)
are etched above the lintel.

## Exits
- [South](go:south) — Crossroads
- [North](go:north) — Ruins Hall

## Items
- A [broken lantern](item:broken-lantern) lies in the rubble near the entrance.
