# Forest Edge

Tall oaks and elms press close, their canopy filtering the light to a
green twilight. The road dwindles to a narrow path choked with ferns.
Bird calls echo overhead, but deeper in the woods an uneasy silence waits.
[Claw marks](cmd:examine marks) score the bark of a nearby tree.
