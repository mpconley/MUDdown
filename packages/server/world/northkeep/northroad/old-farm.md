# Old Farm

A weathered farmstead surrounded by a low stone wall. The thatched roof
sags in the middle, and a [scarecrow](cmd:examine scarecrow) lists sideways
in an overgrown field. A few chickens scratch in the dirt near a dry well.
Despite the disrepair, smoke curls from the chimney.
