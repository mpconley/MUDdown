# North Road

A rutted dirt road climbs away from the Iron Gate into rolling, wind-swept
hills. Dry grass rustles on either side, and a wooden signpost points in
several directions. The walls of Northkeep shrink behind you.

## Exits
- [South](go:south) — The Iron Gate
- [North](go:north) — Crossroads
