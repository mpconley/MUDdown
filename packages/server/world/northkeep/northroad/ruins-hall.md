# Ruins Hall

A vast underground chamber with a vaulted ceiling lost in darkness. Fallen
pillars lie across a cracked marble floor. The remains of a great [mosaic](cmd:examine mosaic)
depict a battle between armored knights and something with too many eyes.
Water drips steadily from above, pooling in shallow depressions.

## Exits
- [South](go:south) — Ruins Entrance

## Present
- A [skeletal guardian](npc:skeleton) stands motionless in an alcove, sword in hand.

## Items
- A [chipped shield](item:shield) lies half-buried in rubble.
- A [gold ring](item:gold-ring) gleams in a crack between the floor tiles.
:::
