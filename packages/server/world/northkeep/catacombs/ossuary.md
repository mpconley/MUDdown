# Ossuary

Walls of neatly stacked bones rise from floor to ceiling — skulls arranged
in geometric patterns, femurs interlocked like brickwork. The craftsmanship
is unsettling in its precision. A faint draft carries the smell of damp
earth from somewhere deeper. A [stone plaque](cmd:examine plaque) bears a
warning in an old tongue.

- [North](go:north) — Sealed Chamber
