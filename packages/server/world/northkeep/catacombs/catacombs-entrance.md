# Catacombs Entrance

A narrow stone staircase descends from the temple into cool, still air.
Niches line the walls, each holding a shrouded bundle. Iron sconces jut from
the stone between them, casting dancing shadows. The [inscriptions](cmd:examine inscriptions)
beside each niche record names and dates stretching back centuries.

## Exits
- [Up](go:up) — Temple of the Silver Moon
- [North](go:north) — Ossuary

## Items
- A [tallow candle](item:candle) burns low in its sconce.
:::
