# Sealed Chamber

A heavy stone door, now open a crack, reveals a circular chamber carved
from living rock. [Glyphs](cmd:examine glyphs) pulse faintly on the walls, their
light too dim to read by. At the center stands a stone sarcophagus, its lid
slightly ajar. The silence here is absolute — even your breathing sounds muffled.

## Exits
- [South](go:south) — Ossuary

## Items
- A [dust-covered tome](item:tome) rests on a stone pedestal.
- A [silver amulet](item:silver-amulet) hangs from the sarcophagus lid.
:::
